Essential infrastructure for the modern firm

Intake that signs cases

Speed to lead decides who gets the case. We build intake systems with instant routing, automated follow-up, and e-signature workflows — so qualified leads become signed clients before your competitors return the call.

Case tracking and analytics

Know exactly where every case sits, how long each phase takes, and which practice areas actually drive profit. Real-time dashboards replace the end-of-month spreadsheet scramble.

Permissions and accountability

Paralegals, associates, and partners each see what they need — nothing more. Role-based access, task ownership, and audit trails keep every case moving and every hand-off clean.

Practice management integration

FileVine, Clio, Litify — we connect your practice management platform to intake, marketing, and reporting so data flows one way: forward. No double entry, no lost records.
