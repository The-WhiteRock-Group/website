Value creation is a journey of many steps. The first is selecting operators who can consolidate systems, migrate data, and align teams without risking revenue. Few decisions matter more.

Set a course for growth

We provide support at each crucial stage of the portfolio lifecycle from due diligence to exit ready.

Financial diligence tells you what the business has done. Operational diligence tells you what it will take to do more.

We run a structured pre-acquisition assessment across five operating dimensions; strategic clarity, leadership capability, organizational focus, execution discipline, and cultural health. Each is rated on two axes: how important it is to the specific growth thesis, and how well the business is actually performing against it. The gap between those two numbers is the risk.

This includes:

  • A heat map of operating risk by dimension, coded by severity
  • A talent dependency analysis
  • Go-to-market readiness assessment on whether the revenue motion is repeatable and transferable
  • A technology and systems audit about what the current stack can support, where the gaps are, and what investment the growth plan will require

Connect the tools your portfolio companies already use into a unified data layer. We eliminate redundant systems, fix broken integrations, and create the technical foundation for automation.

Clean, structured data pipelines that give operators and investors a single source of truth. From pipeline analytics to board-ready reporting — built for decisions, not dashboards.

Repeatable processes for customer acquisition, expansion, and retention. We install the operational playbooks that let portfolio companies scale without scaling headcount at the same rate.
