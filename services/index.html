Operations & Technology Due Diligence

We help investors and operators understand whether the growth story is real, fragile, or fixable. Our work uncovers the revenue, customer, sales, technology, and leadership constraints that can quietly erode value before or after investment. Our high-impact operational capabilities, state-of-the-art tools, and unrivaled expertise provide operational and technology due diligence under one roof.

  • Revenue operations assessment
  • Customer and market signal review
  • Sales motion and buying friction analysis
  • Pipeline quality assessment
  • Objection and win/loss analysis
  • "Do nothing" loss analysis
  • AI and technology readiness
  • CRM, data, and reporting review
