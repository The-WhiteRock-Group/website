About The WhiteRock Group

The WhiteRock Group is a Revenue Value Creation™ firm based in Dallas, Texas. We partner with private equity firms, founders, and B2B service companies to scale revenue, build go-to-market capability, and create lasting enterprise value. Founded in 2015, the firm operates at the intersection of strategy, systems, and execution.

The WhiteRock Group helps ambitious leadership teams build the systems, structure, and commercial capability required to scale and endure. Our work spans revenue strategy, CRM enablement, sales enablement, go-to-market execution, and AI-powered revenue systems — delivered as embedded partners, not outside consultants.

The WhiteRock Group is headquartered in Dallas, Texas, and works with clients across the United States.

Revenue Value Creation™ is The WhiteRock Group's proprietary framework for building durable revenue growth. It aligns brand, revenue operations, sales enablement, and go-to-market execution around a single objective: creating lasting enterprise value. It is designed for companies preparing for growth, integrating after investment, or building a more valuable business before exit.

The WhiteRock Group operates as an embedded partner rather than a traditional consulting firm. We step onto the field with companies — working alongside leadership teams to build and execute, not just advise. The difference is accountability: we are measured by outcomes, not deliverables.

The WhiteRock Group was founded in 2015 in Dallas, Texas. Over the past decade, the firm has partnered with global brands, private equity-backed companies, and founder-led businesses across B2B services, health and wellness, AI and technology, and beyond.

Services

The WhiteRock Group offers Revenue Value Creation™, Go-To-Market Strategy and Sales Enablement, AI Enablement and Revenue Systems, and Portfolio and Founder Growth Advisory. Each engagement is tailored to the specific growth stage, operational context, and value creation objectives of the client.

Go-To-Market strategy at The WhiteRock Group covers the full commercial architecture — market positioning, ideal customer profile definition, channel strategy, sales motion design, and revenue team enablement. We build GTM systems that are executable, not just theoretical.

The WhiteRock Group helps companies deploy AI across the revenue function — automating pipeline management, enriching CRM data, accelerating sales intelligence, and building AI-powered workflows that reduce friction and increase commercial output. We focus on practical implementation, not AI for its own sake.

CRM enablement at The WhiteRock Group means transforming a CRM from a database into an operating system for revenue. We have deep experience with HubSpot and have helped companies use CRM as the connective layer for pipeline visibility, customer understanding, and sales execution.

Operations and Technology Due Diligence is The WhiteRock Group's pre-investment assessment of a target company's commercial infrastructure. We evaluate the strength of the revenue operations, technology stack, GTM process, and team capability — giving investors a clear picture of value creation potential and risk before close.
