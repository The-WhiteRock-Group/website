About Us

We have a few ground rules at The WhiteRock Group.*

*Nothing groundbreaking. Just the things that keep us honest.

The WhiteRock Group was founded in 2015 in Dallas, Texas. In 2015, Facebook, now Meta, asked us to support work tied to its MAU North Star mission. That partnership reaffirmed the belief that still guides us today: The technology is not the thing — the customer experience is.

Next, we partnered with HubSpot to envision, scale, and grow CRM service offerings that helped founders and teams become stronger go-to-market operators. Today, HubSpot serves more than 288,000 customers in over 135 countries.

Over time, we saw the same pattern repeat: growth creates complexity. Sales gets disconnected from operations. Technology gets implemented without adoption. Customer experience becomes fragmented.

We started the private equity division to provide Revenue Value Creation for PE groups and founder-led brands by bridging due diligence, integrations, and enablement under one roof. Today, we span two continents with a tight-knit team and a network of world-class experts.

What We Stand On

01

Be so good they talk about you at the dinner table.

We do not aim to simply satisfy clients. We aim to create the kind of value, trust, and transformation that gets talked about when we are not in the room. Value comes first. The client is the hero. The process is not the thing — the customer outcome is.

02

Built different on purpose.

We believe in simple, but not simplistic. Strategy, revenue operations, sales enablement, brand, customer experience, and leadership alignment — all under one roof. Because growth is connected, and the firms that treat it that way win.

03

High standards are contagious.

Culture is not what we say. Culture is what we tolerate, repeat, reward, and protect. We admire the people we hire. We defend against complacency, bureaucracy, and "Day 2" thinking. Talent is worth over-investing in.

04

Pressure is a privilege.

Our team comes from professional sports, private equity, revenue leadership, brand building, NASA, operations, and entrepreneurship. We think clearly. We move deliberately. We operate with intention. Founders choose us for the consistency of our people, our process, and our standard.

Our customers, our clients, our partners, and our people are our stakeholders. We consider them in every decision that we make.
The WhiteRock Group

Got questions? We can probably answer them.

Where does the name "WhiteRock" come from?

White Rock Lake in Dallas, Texas. It is where the company was conceived — a place where founders go to think, run, and plan what comes next. The name is a reminder that the best ideas start with clarity and space.

What does "Revenue Value Creation" mean?

It means aligning every revenue-facing system — brand, sales, operations, technology, customer experience — so they compound into enterprise value, not just top-line growth. It is the difference between revenue that looks good on a dashboard and revenue that holds up under diligence.

Who do you work with?

Founders, private equity firms, and B2B service companies between $1M and $50M in revenue. Typically at a growth inflection — preparing for investment, integrating after acquisition, or professionalizing the revenue engine before exit.

Where are you based?

Dallas, Texas. But we work across two continents with a distributed team and a network of world-class partners. The work goes where the clients are.
